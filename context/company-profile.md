## Current Pain Points

**Why AI Agent is Needed:**
1. **Volume Overload:** 500+ support tickets per day
2. **Repetitive Questions:** 60% are routine queries (password resets, feature questions, billing inquiries)
3. **24/7 Coverage Gap:** Human agents can't cover all timezones
4. **Response Time:** Customers expect <5 minute responses
5. **Cost:** Human support is expensive and doesn't scale
6. **Multi-channel Complexity:** Customers contact via email, WhatsApp, and web form - hard to track

**Expected AI Agent Impact:**
- Handle 70% of routine queries automatically
- Reduce response time to <2 minutes
- Free up human agents for complex issues
- Provide true 24/7 coverage across all channels
- Reduce support costs by 40%
- Maintain conversation context across channels
