## 1. Order Management

### How to Place an Order
1. Browse products and add items to cart
2. Click "Checkout" when ready
3. Enter shipping address
4. Select payment method
5. Review order and confirm
6. You'll receive order confirmation via email

### Order Tracking
- Track your order at: www.custora.ai/track-order
- Enter your order number and email address
- Real-time tracking updates via email and SMS
- Tracking number provided within 24 hours of shipment

### Order Status Meanings
- **Processing**: Order received, preparing for shipment
- **Shipped**: Package handed to carrier
- **In Transit**: Package on the way to you
- **Out for Delivery**: Package will arrive today
- **Delivered**: Package successfully delivered

### How to Cancel an Order
- Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement
- Go to "My Orders" and click "Cancel Order"
- If already shipped, you'll need to initiate a return
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### How to Modify an Order
- Address changes: Contact support within 1 hour of order
- Cannot modify items after order is placed
- Cancel and place new order if needed

### Order Issues
**Wrong Item Received:**
- Contact support immediately with order number and photos
- We'll arrange free return pickup
- Replacement shipped within 24 hours

**Damaged Item:**
- Report within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide photos of damage
- Full refund or replacement offered

**Missing Items:**
- Check all packaging carefully
- Report within 48 hours
- We'll investigate and resolve within 24 hours

## 2. Shipping & Delivery

### Shipping Methods
**Standard Shipping (Free on orders $50+):**
- Delivery: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50

**Express Shipping:**
- Delivery: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99

**Overnight Shipping:**
- Delivery: Next business day
- Cost: $24.99
- Order by 2 PM EST for next-day delivery

### International Shipping
- Available to 100+ countries
- Delivery: 10-15 business days
- Customs fees may apply (customer responsibility)
- Tracking provided for all international orders

### Shipping Restrictions
- P.O. Boxes: Standard shipping only
- APO/FPO addresses: Supported with standard shipping
- Some products cannot ship internationally (electronics with batteries)

### Delivery Address
- Ensure address is complete and accurate
- Include apartment/unit numbers
- Provide phone number for delivery coordination
- Address changes must be requested within 1 hour

### Delivery Issues
**Package Not Delivered:**
- Check with neighbors and building management
- Verify delivery address was correct
- Contact carrier with tracking number
- We'll investigate and reship if needed

**Delivery Delayed:**
- Check tracking for updates
- Weather and holidays may cause delays
- Contact support if delayed more than 2 days past estimate

## 3. Returns & Exchanges

### Return Policy
- **30-day return window** from delivery date
- Items must be unused, unworn, and in original packaging
- Original tags must be attached
- Return shipping: Free for defective items, $7.99 for other returns

### Non-Returnable Items
- Intimate apparel and swimwear
- Personalized/custom items
- Digital products
- Beauty products (opened)
- Final sale items

### How to Return an Item
1. Go to "My Orders" and select "Return Item"
2. Choose reason for return
3. Print return label (emailed to you)
4. Pack item securely with all original materials
5. Drop off at any carrier location
6. Refund processed within 5-7 days of receiving return

### Exchange Process
- We don't offer direct exchanges
- Return original item for refund
- Place new order for desired item
- Faster processing than exchange

### Refund Timeline
- Refund initiated within 2 business days of receiving return
- Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
- PayPal refunds: 2-3 business days
- Original shipping charges non-refundable (unless item defective)

### Defective/Damaged Returns
- Free return shipping provided
- Full refund including original shipping
- Replacement shipped immediately upon request
- No need to wait for return to arrive

## 5. Account Management

### Creating an Account
- Click "Sign Up" on homepage
- Provide email and create password
- Verify email address
- Start shopping!

### Account Benefits
- Faster checkout
- Order history tracking
- Saved addresses and payment methods
- Wishlist functionality
- Exclusive member discounts

### Password Reset
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link (valid 1 hour)
4. Create new password
5. Password must be 8+ characters with number and symbol

### Update Account Information
- Go to "My Account" → "Profile"
- Update name, email, phone number
- Add/edit shipping addresses
- Manage payment methods

### Email Preferences
- Manage subscriptions in "My Account" → "Email Preferences"
- Choose frequency of promotional emails
- Opt-in for SMS notifications
- Unsubscribe anytime

### Account Security
- Use strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
- Never share password with anyone
- Log out on shared devices

### Delete Account
- Contact support to request account deletion
- All personal data removed within 30 days
- Order history retained for legal compliance (7 years)
